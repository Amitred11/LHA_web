Origin Story
No Spoilers

LHA wasn't built in a boardroom. It was built in School, and the desire to make cool things with cool people.

The Spark

It Started in Class

Just a group chat of BSIT students who realized school projects were boring. We wanted to build apps that people actually used.

The Pivot

Breaking The Loop

Freelancing is lonely. We decided to pool our resources, share clients, and critique each other's code. A collective was born.

Present Day

The Ecosystem

Now, LHA is a hub for developers, designers, and gamers. We build, we play, and we help each other level up.

The Mission

To build exceptional digital experiences without the corporate fluff, fostering a space where "learning by doing" is the only rule that matters.

The Vision

To be the go-to collective for creative tech talent. If you need something built, you call LHA. If you want to learn how to build, you join LHA.
