LHA was born from a shared passion for building amazing things and a desire to create a community where innovators could thrive together. This is our story.

Our Origin

LHA began as a simple group chat created by BSIT students who dreamed of building apps for their community. What started as a small idea grew into a shared vision.

We grew tired of the transactional feel of freelance work and the isolation of working alone, so we decided to create our own space—somewhere we could build projects together and form meaningful connections. That’s how the LHA Collective was created.
